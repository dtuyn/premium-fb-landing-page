About Me

Bridging Heritage
and The Future.

My journey began in the heart of Germany's culinary scene, evolving through the Michelin-starred kitchens of France to the grand hotel ballrooms of London. For 35 years, I have navigated the complex pulse of the European F&B market.

I specialize in translating traditional craftsmanship into modern operational success. My approach is rooted in the rigorous standards of the German hospitality tradition, blended with a contemporary understanding of global dining trends.
